About Us

Founded in 2015, Boteco is India’s first and only authentic Brazilian restaurant chain. We bring the vibrant spirit of Brazil to life through bold, innovative cuisine, a warm sense of community, and a lively atmosphere inspired by the streets of Rio. From tropical plants to mosaic murals, every detail reflects Brazil’s rich cultural heritage. At the heart of Boteco is our Executive Chef, Guto Souza, whose culinary journey spans over 30 years. With deep roots in traditional Brazilian cooking, Chef Guto brings authenticity, passion, and innovation to every dish we serve.
